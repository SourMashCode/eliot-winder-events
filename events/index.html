With roots in the underground festival scene and a career that's evolved through some of the most prestigious stages on the West Coast, I bring a rare combination of technical mastery, creative vision, and operational precision to every production.

Whether it's a $3M institutional season, a sold-out festival weekend, a luxury wedding at the Belmond El Encanto, or a corporate activation for thousands — I bring the same level of care, the same depth of expertise, and the same drive to exceed expectations.
