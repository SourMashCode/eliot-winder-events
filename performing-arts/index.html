About
The Producer Behind the Curtain

As Production Manager for UC Santa Barbara Arts & Lectures — the largest performing arts presenter between Los Angeles and San Francisco — I oversee every dimension of production for one of the most ambitious cultural seasons on the West Coast.

From negotiating artist riders with world-renowned musicians to managing a $3M+ annual production budget across 11 venues, I bring institutional rigor, technical depth, and a genuine passion for the arts to every event.
